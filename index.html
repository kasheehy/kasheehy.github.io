About

I am interested in how dominance hierarchies affect movement decisions in animal groups. I have built a high resolution tracking system to follow movement and behavioral interactions between fish. This allows me to track who is biting who, who is associating with who, and how the structure of hierarchies change over time.

Research

It's not what you know, it's who you know?

I work with the Amazon molly, a naturally genetically clonal species of fish. And while she is an emerging model system POWERHOUSE, there is still a lot we don't know about these fish! My first dissertation chapter disentangles the relative contributions of size and social experience to a fish's position within a hierarchy.
