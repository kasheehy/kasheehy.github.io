About Kirsten

Academic Interests

I am interested in how dominance hierarchies affect movement decisions in animal groups. I have built a high resolution tracking system to follow movement and behavioral interactions between fish. This allows me to track who is biting who, who is associating with who, and how the structure of hierarchies change over time.
